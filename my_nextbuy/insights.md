# EDA 2 — Business Insights

## Insight 1 — Most Reordered Products
Products with the highest reorder rate are everyday essentials 
like bananas, milk, and yogurt. These products are bought 
consistently by customers across multiple orders.

**Business recommendation:** Ensure these products are always 
in stock and prominently displayed.


## Insight 2 — First in Cart
Products with the highest first ratio are the ones customers 
think of first when starting a new order. These are typically 
staple items that drive customers to open the app.

**Business recommendation:** Place these products on the 
homepage and in the first visible section of the app.

## Insight 3 — Co-Purchase Pairs
Certain products are consistently bought together in the same 
order. These pairs represent natural bundles that can be used 
to increase the average basket size.

**Business recommendation:** Create bundle promotions and 
power recommendation engines using these pairs.

## Insight 4 — Department Analysis
Produce and dairy departments generate the most orders and 
have the highest reorder rates. These departments are the 
primary reason customers return to the platform.

**Business recommendation:** Prioritise these departments 
in stock management, pricing strategy, and promotional 
investment.
